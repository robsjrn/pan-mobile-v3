Pan Africa Life Umash Funeral Plan & Funeral Cash Plan

WHY DO I NEED THIS COVERS?

• To provide families with peace of mind in the event of loss of life

• We have the option to provide either benefits or cash for those who don’t wish to use Umash.

UMASH FUNERAL PLAN

WHAT BENEFITS DO I GET?

The Benefits include:

• Hearse – Land Cruiser or trailer

• Coffin - casket

• Mortuary charges

• Flowers

• Obituary – Nation or Standard dailies – 8cm by 2cm colour

• Complimentary services

WHAT ARE COMPLEMENTARY SERVICES?

• Collection of the deceased from place of death to Umash home at no extra cost, within Nairobi and its environs.

• Automated lowering gear, green carpets for grave side, church trolley and PA system

• Red ribbons for family cars and white stickers for the bereaved family.

• Exclusive use of the state of the art chapel at Umash Funeral Home.

HOW DO I PAY MY PREMIUM?

These will be done via M-pesa/Airtel Money

• Payments should be made to Pan Africa Life

• The payments are annual payments.

WHO IS ELIGIBLE?

• For children, we need the birth certificate number

• Principal member – 18 – 60 years

• Spouse – 18 – 60 years

• Children – 0 – 24 years

• Parents – 18 – 80 years

WHAT ARE THE WAITING PERIODS?

• Policyholder, Spouse and Children – 3 months

• Parents – 6 months
