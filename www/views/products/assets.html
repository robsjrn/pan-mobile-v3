Unit Trusts

The Pan Africa Unit Trust scheme offers three exciting funds which you can now access as an investor from as low as Kshs 2,500

The 3 funds allow a choice of investment products that match your investment needs. Unit Trusts work by pooling contributions of many investors into one large fund (pooled fund) which can then be invested in a variety of financial products, including money market, treasury and corporate bonds, shares and property. These funds are a good way to invest. This is due to the nature of ‘collective investments’ which spreads your money around, and lowers the risk of loss of your investment, which benefits from the expertise of fund management
